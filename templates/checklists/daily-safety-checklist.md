## Site Conditions

- [ ] Work areas clear of debris and hazards
- [ ] Proper signage and barricades in place
- [ ] Adequate lighting in all work areas
- [ ] Emergency exits accessible and marked

## Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

- [ ] Hard hats worn in required areas
- [ ] Safety glasses/goggles available and in use
- [ ] High-visibility vests worn on site
- [ ] Steel-toed boots required and enforced
- [ ] Hearing protection in high-noise zones

## Fall Protection

- [ ] Guardrails in place at open edges
- [ ] Harnesses inspected and properly worn
- [ ] Ladder safety protocols followed
- [ ] Floor openings covered and marked

## Electrical Safety

- [ ] GFCI protection on all temporary power
- [ ] Lockout/tagout procedures followed
- [ ] Extension cords in good condition
- [ ] Electrical panels accessible (3 ft clearance)

## Fire Prevention

- [ ] Fire extinguishers available and inspected
- [ ] Hot work permits obtained where required
- [ ] Flammable materials stored properly
- [ ] No smoking in restricted areas
